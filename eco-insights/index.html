♻️Waste-to-Value🪙

Recycling wastes is significant when it comes to saving resources, it helps our planet in many practical ways:

  • 1 ton of plastic bottles -> 0.7 tons of reusable material
  • 1 ton of scrap steel -> 0.9 tons of new steel
  • Recycling steel saves 47% production cost
  • Recycling steel reduces water pollution & solid waste by 97%

🌿Biodiversity Protection🌎

Protecting biodiversity is important for maintaining the balance of life and environment:

  • Biodiversity includes all living organisms
  • Maintaining biodiversity supports stability of the ecosystems
  • Ecosystems with rich biodiversity supports many aspects of our lives, such as food avaliability, medicinal values, scientific contributions, and culture formations.

⚡Renewable Energy💧

Renewable energy are energy that come from natural resources that won't run out and cause less harm to the planet compare to fossil fuels:

  • Solar energy: uses sunlight to generate electricity
  • Wind energy: uses wind turbines to control air flow to create power
  • Hydropower: uses flowing rivers to spin turbines and generate electricity
